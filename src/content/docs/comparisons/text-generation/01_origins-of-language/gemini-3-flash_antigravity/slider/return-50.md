### Thesis: The Resilient Germanic Lineage
The standard historical narrative traces English through a relatively linear, though turbulent, progression of four main stages:

*   **Late Modern English (1800–Present):** British colonialism and the Industrial Revolution turned English into a global lingua franca, incorporating loanwords from hundreds of languages and branching into diverse international dialects.
