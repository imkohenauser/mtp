### The Thesis: Wonderland as a Charming Escape

Suppose the opposite: *Wonderland* is not a whimsical escape from adult rules, but a devastating satire of them. In this light, the book is a psychological battleground. Carroll, a mathematician and logician, uses formal logic to prove that our everyday conventions, manners, and laws are completely absurd when pushed to their logical limits. It is a brilliant, slightly unsettling exploration of the anxiety of losing control.
