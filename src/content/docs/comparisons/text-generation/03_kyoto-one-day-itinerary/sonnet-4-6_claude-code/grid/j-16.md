Start at **Fushimi Inari Taisha** the moment it opens (the shrine itself is open 24 hours, but arriving by 7 a.m. lets you walk the lower torii-gate tunnels before tour groups arrive). By late morning the heat begins in earnest, which makes the stone-paved lanes of **Nishiki Market** in central Kyoto a welcome next stop — narrow, covered, and alive with vendors of pickled vegetables, fresh tofu, and grilled skewers.
