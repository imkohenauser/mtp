    *   **Cost:** 880 JPY per adult.
4.  **12:30 – Hozugawa River Boat Ride**
    *   **Action:** Return to central Arashiyama via a traditional 2-hour boat descent.
    *   **Why:** This is the ultimate cooling strategy; the spray from the rapids and the river breeze provide active relief from the sun.
    *   **Cost:** 6,000 JPY per adult.
5.  **14:30 – Yudofu (Boiled Tofu) Lunch**
