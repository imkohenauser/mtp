This itinerary focuses on the **Kibune and Kurama** area, a mountain retreat in northern Kyoto that is consistently 5–10°C cooler than the city center. It centers on the traditional summer experience of *Kawadoko* (riverbed dining) and the sacred forest hike.

#### **Critical Advance Verifications**

1.  **Kawadoko Reservations (High Priority):**
    *   **Availability:** *Kawadoko* dining is seasonal, typically operating from **May 1st to September 30th**.
    *   **Booking:** Popular restaurants like *Hirobun* (known for *Nagashi Somen* or flowing noodles) do not take reservations for somen and can have 2–3 hour waits. For full *Kaiseki* meals, reservations must be made at least 2–4 weeks in advance via the restaurant's website or phone.
