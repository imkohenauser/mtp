Alternatively, the **Kyoto Botanical Gardens** often have seasonal summer displays and shaded paths if you prefer to stay outdoors.

**Evening: Gion and Pontocho**
As the sun begins to set, a stroll through the **Gion district** or along the narrow **Pontocho Alley** offers a classic Kyoto evening.
