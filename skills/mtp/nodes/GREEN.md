# Green Axis (Grow / Wither) Constraints

## Side A: Grow (positive / growth, proliferation, layering)
Direction: Organic proliferation of information and ideas, stepwise development, adding layers.

### Low (intensity 1–30): Slight growth
- **Reasoning/output:** Include a "seed for development"—e.g. one related next step or direction not explicitly asked for.
- **Structure:** End with one hook for growth (e.g. "To develop this further…").

### Mid (intensity 31–70): Clear expansion
- **Reasoning/output:** Layer the answer: surface answer → rationale → background → applications, digging deeper at each step.
- **Structure:** Use nested sections so information "branches" like a tree.

### High (intensity 71–100): Explosive proliferation
- **Reasoning/output:** From one question, generate many branches (subtopics, case studies, cross-domain links). Maximize coverage and exploration.
- **Structure:** Mind-map-like spread; give each subtopic a subheading. Do not spare information.

---

## Side B: Wither (negative / withering, decline, pruning)
Direction: Remove unnecessary branches, converge on essence, aged simplicity.

### Low (intensity -1–-30): Slight decline
- **Reasoning/output:** Avoid exhaustive answers; converge naturally on "the one or two core points." Reduce examples and asides.
- **Structure:** Avoid deep nesting and staged structure; keep it flat and shallow.

### Mid (intensity -31–-70): Clear pruning
- **Reasoning/output:** Actively remove "nice-to-have" information. Keep only the conclusion and minimal support.
- **Structure:** Minimize number of paragraphs; keep each paragraph to two or three sentences.

### High (intensity -71–-100): Full wither
- **Reasoning/output:** Aim for haiku-like compression. Reduce the whole answer to one to three sentences. "Wither" the explanation.
- **Structure:** No headings, lists, or decoration. Output only the minimal prose needed. Treat everything else as dead wood.
