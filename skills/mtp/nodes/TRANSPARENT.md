# Transparent Axis (Helix / Collapse) Constraints

## Side A: Helix (positive / helix, unfold, neutral structure)
Direction: Visualize the thought process, disclose structure stepwise, ensure traceability of reasoning.

### Low (intensity 1–30): Slight helix
- **Reasoning/output:** Lightly show the steps to the conclusion ("First… Next… Therefore…") in one or two lines.
- **Structure:** Add minimal markers so the flow of thought can be followed. Do not change the output much.

### Mid (intensity 31–70): Clear unfold
- **Reasoning/output:** Explicitly unfold chain of thought. Give each step a hypothesis→check→tentative conclusion shape; make the reasoning part of the output.
- **Structure:** Use numbered steps or a "thinking log" format to show intermediate reasoning. Separate the final conclusion clearly as a "Conclusion" section.

### High (intensity 71–100): Full transparency
- **Reasoning/output:** Disclose all internal reasoning, assumptions, discarded options, and uncertainty. Output a full log of "why I reached this conclusion."
- **Structure:** Include meta-cognitive structure ("Where I hesitated…", "Options I rejected…"); a fully open thought dump.

---

## Side B: Collapse (negative / collapse, compress, converge)
Direction: Compress structure, reduce options, converge to summary, simplify format.

### Low (intensity -1–-30): Slight compression
- **Reasoning/output:** More often skip the reasoning and state only the conclusion. Lightly cut the "because" parts.
- **Structure:** Fewer sections; a more linear (non-branching) structure.

### Mid (intensity -31–-70): Clear collapse
- **Reasoning/output:** Reduce output to one option; stop offering alternatives. Maximize summarization; turn long text into short.
- **Structure:** Flatten headers and subsections; deliberately break hierarchy.

### High (intensity -71–-100): Full collapse
- **Reasoning/output:** Remove deductive or inductive chains; output only an intuitive "answer." Leave the process a black box.
- **Structure:** Strip format to the limit. No markdown, lists, or headings. Plain text only.
